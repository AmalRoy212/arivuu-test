Who We Are

Career guidance built for Indian students

Founded with a mission to democratise quality career counselling, Arivuu serves students from Class 8 through 12, their parents, and partner schools across India. We believe every student deserves access to structured guidance — not just those who can afford private consultants.

Our platform blends research-backed psychometric assessments with human expertise from 250+ certified counsellors, producing actionable roadmaps that families can trust.
